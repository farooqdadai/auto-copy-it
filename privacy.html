Privacy Policy for Auto Copy It

Last Updated: July 2025
Extension Version: 1.0
Effective Date: July 2025
Important: This extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All functionality is performed within your browser.

1. Introduction

Auto Copy It ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension. This extension is designed with privacy-first principles and operates entirely locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Preferences and Settings

We store your extension settings locally on your device using Chrome's storage API, including:

  • Copy format preferences (plain text, HTML, uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case)
  • Notification settings (position, duration, style)
  • Sound notification preferences
  • Copy delay settings
  • Blacklisted domains (websites where auto-copy is disabled)
  • Consent requirement settings
  • Permission status (whether you've granted website access)

2.2 Copy History (Optional and Disabled by Default)

If you explicitly enable the "Store Copy History" feature, we may store a limited history of copied text locally on your device. This feature is:

  • Disabled by default - No history is stored unless you enable it
  • Limited to the last 10 copied items maximum
  • Stored only locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • Never transmitted to any external servers
  • Can be disabled at any time through the extension settings

2.3 Permission Status Tracking

We track whether you have granted the extension permission to access websites and clipboard functionality. This information is stored locally and used only to:

  • Enable/disable features appropriately
  • Show correct status in the extension popup
  • Remember your permission preferences

2.4 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Browsing history or website URLs
  • Text content you copy (unless you explicitly enable copy history)
  • Any data to external servers or third parties
  • Analytics or tracking information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the auto-copy functionality you requested
  • Remember your preferences and settings across browser sessions
  • Show appropriate notifications when text is copied
  • Respect your blacklist settings for specific domains
  • Ensure the extension works only when you have granted necessary permissions
  • Format copied text according to your preferences

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any external services.

4.2 No Server Communication

Our extension does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. All functionality operates entirely within your browser and on your device.

4.3 Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists as long as the extension is installed. When you uninstall the extension, all stored data is automatically removed by Chrome. You can also clear specific data through the extension settings.

4.4 Data Security

Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security features and is stored using Chrome's secure storage API. We do not implement additional security measures as all data remains on your device.

5. Permissions and Access

5.1 Required Permissions

  • Storage: To save your preferences and settings locally
  • Active Tab: To access the current webpage for text selection
  • Scripting: To inject content scripts only after permission is granted

5.2 Optional Permissions (Requested with Explicit Consent)

  • Host Permissions: To enable auto-copy on websites you visit (requested only when you click "Enable Auto-Copy on All Sites")
  • Clipboard Write: To copy text to your clipboard (requested only with your explicit consent)

5.3 Permission Management

You can revoke any permissions at any time through Chrome's extension management page. The extension will stop functioning if you revoke host permissions.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data and the extension's functionality:

  • Permission Control: You can grant or revoke permissions at any time
  • Feature Control: You can disable auto-copy functionality through settings
  • History Control: You can enable/disable copy history storage
  • Data Deletion: You can clear stored data through extension settings
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all stored data
  • Consent Control: You can require consent for each copy operation

7. Third-Party Services and Dependencies

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking tools, or external dependencies. The extension operates independently without any external communication.

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this extension.

9. International Users

This extension is designed to operate locally on your device. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, international data transfer regulations do not apply to our service.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: farooqdaadkhan@gmail.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/farooqdadai/auto-copy
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours

12. Legal Basis and Compliance

This extension operates under the legal basis of legitimate interest, providing a useful service while maintaining user privacy. We comply with applicable privacy laws and Chrome Web Store policies.

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the Auto Copy It Chrome extension. By using this extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
